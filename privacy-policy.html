Privacy Policy for QuickWhats Extension

Last updated: September 1, 2025

Data Collection

QuickWhats does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

Local Storage

The extension only stores user preferences (such as country code settings and message templates) locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

Phone Numbers

QuickWhats processes phone numbers locally in your browser to generate WhatsApp links. No phone numbers are stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The extension opens WhatsApp Web (wa.me) links but does not share any data with WhatsApp or any other services.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.

Contact
